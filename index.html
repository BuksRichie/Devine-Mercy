About Us

We are a non-profit organization committed to reducing illiteracy in remote areas of Uganda by providing free basic education to children, youths and adults who cannot read, write, or count and have no alternative options of changing their situation without special intervention.

We are also dedicated to supplementing the educational experiences of children in the free public schools of Kambaala village by providing instruction in subject areas, reading, and writing materials for individual and communal use, and time and place to support spontaneous learning and practice skills.

We hope you will support our efforts in any way possible.

Thank you.
